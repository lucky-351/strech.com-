Neck Pain
Side Rotation

Firstly Keep your head squarely over your shoulders and your back straight.

Then Slowly turn your head to the right until you feel tension in the side of your neck and shoulder.

Then Hold the stretch for 15-30 seconds, and then slowly turn your head forward again.Repeat on your left side. Do up to 10 sets.

Back Pain
Child pose

Start with your hands and knees on the ground, extend back through your hips to rest them on your heels.

Then hinge at your hips as you fold forward, walking your hands out in front of you. After that rest your stomach on your thighs.

Then extend your arms in front of or alongside your body with your palms facing up.

Focus on breathing deeply and relaxing any areas of tension or tightness.

Bicep and Tricep pain
wall stretch

press your left palm against a wall or a sturdy object.

Slowly turn your body away from the wall. Feel the stretch in your chest, shoulder, and arm.

Hold this position for up to 30 seconds. Repeat on the opposite side.

Abdominal Pain
Cobra Stretch

Lay your stomach flat on the floor.

Then lift your spine up as shown in the picture.

Hold this position for 30 seconds while looking up in the sky.

Thigh Pain
Lying Hamstring Stretch

Lie on your back on the floor.

Slowly lift one leg and hold it extended upward.

Pull on your hamstring so that the leg raise is at a 90-degree angle from your torso and bend your leg at the knee.

Hold the position for several seconds, then repeat with the other leg.

Calf Pain
Calf Stretch

Stand near a wall with one foot in front of the other, front knee slightly bent.

Keep your back knee straight, your heel on the ground, and lean toward the wall.

Feel the stretch all along the calf of your back leg.Hold this stretch for 20-30 seconds.
